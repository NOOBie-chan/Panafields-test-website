Who We Are

Our Story

Founded in 2004 in Ghana, Panafields has grown into a trusted Pan-African field research and data collection partner. From humble beginnings with a small but dedicated team, we have expanded operations across West, East and Central Africa, delivering reliable insights for multinational agencies, NGOs and local organizations alike.

Our journey has always been guided by integrity, precision and cultural understanding. Every survey, focus group and field operation we undertake reflects our commitment to delivering actionable insights that drive meaningful decisions.

📈

Our Mission

To provide a consistent, reliable, professional and timely service to organizations requiring information on any aspect of the social, economic and marketing issues in Africa.

🌎

Our Vision

To be the leading African company in providing, economic, social and marketing data, in accordance with best international practice.

Our Services

Focus Group Discussion

Our focus group discussions are designed to provide deep insights into consumer attitudes, perceptions and behaviors. We facilitate dynamic conversations that uncover the underlying motivations driving consumer decisions.

Extended / In Depth Interviews (EDIs)

Our EDIs offer a comprehensive exploration of individual perspectives, allowing us to delve into the nuances of consumer experiences and preferences. Through one-on-one interviews, we gather rich qualitative data that informs strategic decision-making.

Face-to-Face Interviews

Our face-to-face interviews provide an opportunity for direct engagement with consumers, enabling us to capture authentic responses and observe non-verbal cues. This method allows for a deeper understanding of consumer needs and preferences.

Telephone Interviews

Our telephone interviews offer a convenient and efficient way to reach a wide range of consumers across different regions. We conduct structured interviews that yield valuable insights while maintaining the flexibility to adapt to respondents' schedules.

Online Interviews

Our online interviews leverage digital platforms to connect with consumers in a virtual environment. This method allows us to reach a diverse audience and gather insights in real-time, providing a comprehensive understanding of consumer behaviors and preferences.

Product Testing

Our product testing services enable us to evaluate consumer reactions to new products, concepts and prototypes. We conduct rigorous testing that provides actionable feedback, helping our clients refine their offerings and ensure market success.

Advert / Concept Evaluation

Our advert and concept evaluation services help our clients assess the effectiveness of their marketing campaigns and product concepts. We provide insights into consumer perceptions, preferences and potential areas for improvement.

Customer Intercept

Our customer intercept services allow us to engage with consumers in real-time at points of purchase or service. This method provides immediate feedback on consumer experiences, preferences and satisfaction levels.

Customer Satisfaction Measurement (CSM)

Our CSM services help our clients gauge customer satisfaction levels and identify areas for improvement. We conduct comprehensive surveys that provide actionable insights into customer experiences and expectations.

Media Survey

Our media survey services provide insights into consumer media consumption habits, preferences and attitudes. We help our clients understand how their target audience engages with different media channels, enabling them to optimize their marketing strategies.

Desk Surveys

Our desk surveys are conducted in person at designated locations, allowing us to gather detailed feedback from consumers in a controlled environment. This method ensures high response rates and provides in-depth insights into consumer preferences and behaviors.

Mystery Shopping

Our mystery shopping services provide an unbiased assessment of customer service, product quality and overall consumer experience. We deploy trained evaluators to interact with our clients' businesses, providing valuable feedback that helps improve customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.

Retail Audit

Our retail audit services offer a comprehensive evaluation of retail environments, including product placement, pricing strategies and promotional activities. We provide actionable insights that help our clients optimize their retail presence and drive sales growth.
